What We Do

South Asian Canadians bear a greater burden of diabetes, as they are twice as likely to develop the disease and its complications, and are diagnosed at a significantly younger age. Poor dietary habits are a key modifiable risk factor that puts South Asians at greater risk for obesity related diseases. Founded by the University of British Columbia, It's a Family Affair strives to change what it means to eat healthy. Through our in-school workshops, online recipes and cooking demonstrations, we hope to conduct a family-focused, “hands-on” model for teaching South Asian children and their parents lifelong strategies for eating and cooking healthy. Make your meals a family affair.
