What We Do

Poor dietary habits are a key modifiable risk factor that puts South Asians at a greater risk for obesity related diseases. Launched by the University of British Columbia, It's a Family Affair works to redefine healthy eating in the South Asian community. By facilitating in-school workshops, providing recipes, and sharing cooking techniques we hope to support families in building healthier eating habits.
