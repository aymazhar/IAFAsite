Much Better Butter Chicken

Indulge your cravings with our delicately seasoned and flavourful butter chicken recipe. Easy to make, tasty, and healthy, this recipe is sure to become a new family favourite.

Directions

    STEP 1
  1. In a medium mixing bowl, add the chopped chicken, grapeseed oil, turmeric, brown sugar, salt and half of the ginger and garlic. Mix together to coat the chicken.
    2. STEP 2
  2. Add the chicken to a non-stick pan over medium heat. Let the chicken cook for about 6 minutes, or until it is no longer pink in the center. Remove the chicken from the pan and place it to the side.
    3. STEP 3
  3. Coat the pan in cooking spray, add onions and the rest of the ginger and garlic. Sautâ€Œé for about 4 minutes or until the onions start to turn golden.
    4. STEP 4
  4. Add the tomato paste, water, chile flakes, cumin seeds, buttermilk, cornstarch and cooked chicken to the pan. Let all the ingredients simmer in the pan for a few minutes or until the sauce thickens. Add in kasuri methi and garam masala to taste. Serve with bulgar wheat.
